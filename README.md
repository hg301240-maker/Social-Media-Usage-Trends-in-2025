# 📱 Social Media Usage Trends in 2025

A data-driven PowerPoint presentation analyzing the latest social media usage trends in 2025. The project explores platform popularity, user demographics, video content growth, business impact, and actionable recommendations using charts, statistics, and visual storytelling.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This presentation provides an overview of how social media is evolving in 2025. It highlights user behavior, platform growth, age-group preferences, video content trends, and the impact of social media on businesses.

The presentation combines statistical analysis with visual charts to make complex information easy to understand.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze global social media usage trends.
- Compare the popularity of major social media platforms.
- Study social media adoption across different age groups.
- Understand the growth of video content.
- Evaluate the impact of social media on businesses.
- Provide recommendations for effective digital marketing strategies.

## 📈 Key Insights

- Over **5.24 Billion** people use social media worldwide.
- Facebook remains the largest platform by active users.
- Short-form video content continues to dominate user engagement.
- Social commerce is becoming a major source of online sales.
- AI-powered content creation and personalized recommendations are shaping the future of social media.
- Younger audiences prefer Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube, while Facebook continues to perform well among older demographics.
